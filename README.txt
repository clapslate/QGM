Description:
QuickGun Murugan is a PC based game. 
It has been tested on Windows XP. Works on almost any PC that can support and has Windows XP.
It does not work on Windows Vista or higher.

Gameplay:
You are Mr. Murugan who is on the hunt for zombies.
Be quick with your mind and your gun; and prove that you deserve to be called 'QuickGun Murugan.'

The game has 2 stages.

-Stage 1

A zombie is trying to escape. You can capture him by correctly answering a math question at the top. 
There are 4 options, out of which one option is the correct answer. Answer correctly before the zombie escapes and progress to the next stage.

-Stage 2
Zombies are going past the screen. Hit them in the head with your gun(mouse) to score points.
With each hit, you score 1 point and the zombies start moving faster across the screen.
With each miss, you lose 3 points.
Score enough points to get listed on the high-score board.


Make sure that the Scores.txt is in the same folder as QGM.exe
